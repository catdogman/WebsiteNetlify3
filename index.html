Ready-Mix Concrete Pump Hire in Surrey, Sussex, Kent and Berkshire

At Embassy Concrete Pumping Limited we specialise in concrete boom pump and static pump hire.

Instead of having to move material around site in a wheelbarrow, our concrete pump can place concrete wherever you want, when you want it and in any quantity that you want it in.

Our trailer pumps and concrete boom pumps are particularly suitable for work in hard-to-access areas or for projects where there is very little street space available for traditional lorry deliveries.

We deliver fully maintained plant and equipment to site and development projects in London, Surrey, Sussex, Kent and all major locations in the South of England.

Since our inception in 1997, we’ve helped to meet increased demand for ready-mix concrete for the construction industry and for small building companies.

Based near Caterham with immediate access to nearby road links, we can deliver concrete pumps and trailer pumps to your projects at fair and competitive prices.

Our equipment meets the exact needs of our customers, who can have ready-mix concrete poured to any quantities on-site. Our pumps can handle deliveries of any size and we offer exact delivery time slots so your own personnel are never left standing around while they wait for plant and equipment to arrive.

We work closely with site managers and business owners in London, Surrey, Sussex and Kent, ensuring they retain complete control over their projects and volumetric concrete.

We have a fully trained team of skilled personnel who operate our concrete pumps to terms and conditions specified by the Construction Plant Hire Association. Following an initial training period, all of our operatives are enrolled to obtain a CPCS Plant Operators Card. Our clients benefit from our stringent training procedures because operatives arrive at site projects fully prepared to meet all concrete pumping requirements.

We can also supply static concrete pumps without operators on a weekly hire.

Get in contact for an instant free quote with the added bonus of same-day and next-day plant deliveries anywhere in London or the surrounding counties of Surrey, Sussex and Kent.
